 About us 

We SIGMATEX INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED, located in Hyderabad, proudly introduce ourselves as a leading and trusted Electrical Panel Builder and Industrial Electrification Contractor. Our expertise spans the design, manufacture, and supply of a comprehensive range of electrical solutions including electrical power distribution panels, process control panels, power factor control panels, AMF panels, synchronizing panels, integrated power control panels, cable trays, and MV & LV Bus Ducts. Additionally, we undertake installation contract works for a diverse array of industries.

Our Journey and Commitment: Established as a reliable and service-oriented organization, we provide seamless services across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. Our state-of-the-art panel manufacturing facility in Miyapur, Hyderabad, is equipped with sophisticated in-house facilities, enabling us to handle all processes under one roof. This integrated approach allows us to meet your complete electrical project requirements efficiently and within specified time frames.
