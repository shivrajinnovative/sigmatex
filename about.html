About Us

We SIGMATEX INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED, located in Hyderabad,

proudly introduce ourselves as a leading and trusted Electrical Panel Builder and Industrial Electrification Contractor. Our expertise spans the design, manufacture, and supply of a comprehensive range of electrical solutions including electrical power distribution panels, process control panels, power factor control panels, AMF panels, synchronizing panels, integrated power control panels, cable trays, and MV & LV Bus Ducts. Additionally, we undertake installation contract works for a diverse array of industries.

Our Philosophy

At SIGMATEX INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED, customer satisfaction is our utmost priority. Our dedicated team of technocrats and professionals ensures value-based performance, delivering high-quality results that align with customer requirements and relevant IS standards. We pride ourselves on our after-sales service, providing round-the-clock support and ensuring complete customer satisfaction in terms of job quality and timely delivery.

Environmental Commitment

Our environmental policy encompasses all our operations, including electrical panel manufacturing and installations, management, office services, delivery, and procurement. The Managing Director ensures the policy's implementation, but all employees are responsible for achieving its aims and objectives in their respective areas.

Our Environment Management System identifies, communicates, and manages environmental issues from initial planning to maintenance completion. We prepare emergency and incident response plans and continuously measure and review performance against corporate key performance indicators, objectives, and targets. Through our Environmental Management System, we commit to:

Defining responsibilities for environmental management.

Auditing and reviewing processes to ensure the continuous improvement of our Environmental Management System.

Preventing and minimizing pollution, dust, noise, vibration, and other nuisances.

Achieving continual improvements in environmental performance.

Minimizing the lifecycle impact of plant, equipment, and other physical assets.

Avoiding and minimizing disruption and damage to local habitats.

Efficiently managing materials and plants to minimize landfill and maximize waste recycling.

Using materials and energy as efficiently as practicable

Communicating appropriate environmental information to all interested parties.

We have implemented Quality Management Systems within our organization to ensure the highest standards of service.
